PAKISTAN

history

The history of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan began on 14 August 1947 when the country came into being in the form of the Dominion of Pakistan within the British Commonwealth as the result of the Pakistan Movement and the partition of India.

location

Pakistan is a country in South Asia, bordered by India to the east, Afghanistan to the west, Iran to the southwest, and China to the northeast, with the Arabian Sea to its south, featuring diverse geography from mountains to plains, with its capital Islamabad in the north and largest city Karachi on the coast.
